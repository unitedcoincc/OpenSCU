# Vision

OpenSCU (Open Settlement Clearing Union) is an open, neutral settlement infrastructure designed to enable asset-referenced trade across regions.

---

## The Problem

Global trade relies on external settlement systems that introduce:

- Dependency on third-party financial infrastructure  
- Exposure to sanctions and restrictions  
- Limited transparency in settlement processes  

---

## The Opportunity

Regions with real assets—energy, infrastructure, production—can support a new settlement model that is:

- Transparent  
- Neutral  
- Anchored in real economic value  

---

## The Vision

OpenSCU aims to create:

- A shared settlement layer for cross-border trade  
- A system where value is referenced to real-world assets  
- An open framework that anyone can audit and contribute to  

---

## Guiding Principles

- Neutrality over control  
- Transparency over opacity  
- Simplicity over complexity  
- Infrastructure over speculation  

---

## Long-Term Goal

To establish a credible, open standard for asset-referenced settlement that can be adopted across regions and institutions.
